# Math Equations Test

## Inline Math
The quadratic formula is $x = \frac{-b \pm \sqrt{b^2 - 4ac}}{2a}$

## Block Math
The Pythagorean theorem states:
$$
a^2 + b^2 = c^2
$$

## Multiple Equations
Here's a system of equations:
$$
\begin{align}
y &= mx + b \\
y &= ax^2 + bx + c
\end{align}
$$

## Complex Math
The integral of $f(x)$ is:
$$
\int_{a}^{b} f(x) \, dx = F(b) - F(a)
$$
