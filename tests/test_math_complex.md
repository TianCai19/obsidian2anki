## Block Equations

$$

$$

## Matrix

$$
\begin{bmatrix}

Definite integral:
$$
\int_{a}^{b} f(x) \, dx = F(b) - F(a)
$$

\sum_{i=1}^{n} i = \frac{n(n+1)}{2}
$$
